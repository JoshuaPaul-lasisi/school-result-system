About Debbyfield Schools

A school community built on the belief that every learner deserves structure, encouragement and the chance to discover their own purpose.

Our Story

Built on community, grown through care

Debbyfield Schools began with a simple conviction — that a school should feel like an extension of home, where children are known by name, encouraged in their strengths, and supported through their struggles.

Today, that conviction shapes two campuses — in Mushin, Lagos and Obafemi-Owode, Ogun — where learners move from Nursery through to Senior Secondary School (SS3) within one consistent, caring system. Along the way, our staff track every learner's attendance, scores, conduct and growth — sharing it openly with parents every term.

At a glance

  • Two campuses — Mushin (Lagos) and Obafemi-Owode (Ogun)
  • Nursery, Primary and Secondary (JSS1 – SS3)
  • Three structured terms each session, with full report cards
  • Dedicated subject teachers and form supervisors for every class
  • Continuous assessment, weekly tests and termly examinations
Mission & Vision

Why Debbyfield exists

Our Mission

To provide a nurturing, disciplined and academically rigorous environment where every learner is equipped — intellectually, morally and socially — to achieve their God-given purpose in life.

Our Vision

To be a reference point for value-driven education — raising graduates who excel in examinations, carry strong character, and go on to make a positive mark on their communities and the world.

What We Stand For

Our Core Values

Excellence

We pursue high standards in academics, conduct and care — and celebrate growth at every level.

Integrity

We teach learners to be honest, accountable and consistent — in the classroom and beyond it.

Compassion

We treat every child and family with warmth, patience and respect — because people come first.

Purpose

We help every learner discover and pursue their own definition of a meaningful, productive life.
