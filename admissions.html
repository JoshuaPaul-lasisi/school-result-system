Join the Debbyfield Family

Admissions are open for Nursery through Senior Secondary across both campuses. Here's everything you need to begin your child's application.

How It Works

A simple, guided application process

1. Enquire

Reach out to our admissions team — by phone, WhatsApp or the form below — to ask questions and confirm space availability.

2. Submit Details

Share your child's information — age, intended class, and any previous school records — so we can guide placement.

3. Assessment & Visit

We arrange a brief placement assessment (where applicable) and welcome you to tour the campus and meet our staff.

4. Confirm & Resume

Once admitted, we'll guide you through registration and fees — and welcome your child to their first day at Debbyfield.

What you'll need

  • Child's birth certificate or age declaration
  • Passport photographs of the child
  • Previous school's report card / testimonial (for transfers)
  • Immunisation record, where available
  • Parent/guardian contact information

Frequently asked

  • Which classes are open? — Nursery through SS3 across both campuses, subject to availability.
  • Do you admit transfer students? — Yes, mid-session transfers are considered with prior records.
  • How are fees structured? — Fees vary by class and term; our admissions team will share the current schedule directly.
  • Can I visit before applying? — Absolutely — campus visits are warmly welcomed; just reach out to schedule one.
